ABOUT DEWI

Passionate about paper folding for 10 years, Dewi Brunet has developed strong skills and knowledge of modern origami. He exhibits his work, organizes workshops, creates interior design and contributes to events. He creates and sells lamps, jewelries, design objects and flower compositions. He is always eager to teach paper folding and share the multiple bridges between origami, science, technology and fashion. You can take direct contact with him for any project or idea you would like to implement, or visit his Instagram or Facebook page for an overview of his work.
